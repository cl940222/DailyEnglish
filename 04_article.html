The Boy who cried 'Wolf'

狼來了

Once there was a shepherd boy, who had to look after a flock of sheep. One day, he felt bored and decided to play a trick on the villagers. He shouted, “Help! Wolf! Wolf!”
從前有一個牧羊男孩，他負責照顧一群羊。有一天，他感到無聊，決定給村民玩個把戲。他大喊：“救命！狼來了！”
The villagers heard his cries and rushed out of the village to help the shepherd boy. When they reached him, they asked, “Where is the wolf?”
村民們聽到他的呼喊，匆忙從村子跑出來幫助牧羊男孩。當他們到達時，他們問：“狼在哪裡？”
The shepherd boy laughed loudly, “Ha, Ha, Ha! I fooled you all. I was only playing a trick on you.”
牧羊男孩大聲笑著說：“哈哈哈！我騙了你們。我只是在逗你們玩。”
A few days later, the shepherd boy played the trick again.
幾天後，牧羊男孩又玩起了同樣的把戲。
He cried, “Help! Help! Wolf! Wolf!” Again, the villagers rushed up the hill to help him and again they found that the boy had tricked them. They were very angry with him for being so naughty.
他喊道：“救命！救命！狼來了！救命！有人幫忙！”村民們再次匆忙上山幫助他，但他們發現男孩又騙了他們一次。他們對他非常生氣，因為他太淘氣了。
Then, sometime later, a wolf really went into the field. The wolf attacked one sheep, and then another and another. The shepherd boy ran towards the village shouting, “Help! Help! Wolf! Help! Somebody!”
後來，有一天，一隻真正的狼進入了牧場。狼襲擊了一隻羊，然後又襲擊了另一隻、再一隻。牧羊男孩跑向村子大喊：“救命！救命！狼來了！救命！有人來幫忙！”
The villagers heard his cries but they laughed because they thought it was just another trick.
村民們聽到他的呼喊，但他們笑了，因為他們以為這只是另一個把戲。
The boy ran to the nearest villager and said, “A wolf is attacking the sheep. I lied before, but this time it is true!”
男孩跑向最近的一位村民，說：“一隻狼正在襲擊羊群。之前我撒了謊，但這次是真的！”
Finally, the villagers went to look. It was true. They could see the wolf running away and many dead sheep lying on the grass.
最終，村民們去看了。果然如此。他們看到狼在逃跑，草地上有許多死去的羊。
We may not believe someone who often tells lies, even when he tells the truth.
我們可能不會相信經常說謊的人，即使他說的是真話。

The Solar System

太陽系

The solar system is an amazing place, full of wonders and mysteries. It consists of the Sun and all the objects that orbit around it, including planets, moons, asteroids, and comets.
太陽系是一個驚人的地方，充滿著奇觀和神秘。它包括太陽和圍繞著它運行的所有物體，包括行星、衛星、小行星和彗星。
At the center of the solar system is the Sun, a massive ball of hot, glowing gas. It provides heat and light to all the planets. The Sun's gravity holds the solar system together.
太陽位於太陽系的中心，是一個巨大的熱氣球。它為所有行星提供熱量和光線。太陽的引力使太陽系保持結合。
There are eight planets in our solar system. Starting from the closest to the Sun, they are Mercury, Venus, Earth, Mars, Jupiter, Saturn, Uranus, and Neptune. Each planet has its unique characteristics and features.
太陽系中有八個行星。從最靠近太陽的行星開始，它們分別是水星、金星、地球、火星、木星、土星、天王星和海王星。每個行星都有其獨特的特點和特徵。
The inner planets, Mercury, Venus, Earth, and Mars, are made mostly of rock and metal. They are known as terrestrial planets. These planets have solid surfaces and are relatively smaller in size compared to the outer planets.
內部行星包括水星、金星、地球和火星，它們主要由岩石和金屬組成。它們被稱為類地行星。這些行星有著固體表面，相對於外部行星而言較小。
Jupiter, Saturn, Uranus, and Neptune are the outer planets, also known as gas giants. They are much larger in size and mainly composed of gas, such as hydrogen and helium. These planets have thick atmospheres and many moons orbiting around them.
木星、土星、天王星和海王星是外部行星，也被稱為氣體巨星。它們體積更大，主要由氫和氦等氣體組成。這些行星有著厚厚的大氣層，並有許多衛星圍繞著它們運行。
Earth, the third planet from the Sun, is the home to a diverse range of life forms, including humans. It has a suitable atmosphere and conditions for supporting life. Earth's moon, called the Moon, is the only natural satellite orbiting our planet.
地球是距離太陽第三近的行星，是各種生命形式，包括人類的家園。它具有適合支持生命的大氣和條件。地球的衛星，稱為月球，是環繞我們的行星的唯一自然衛星。
Apart from planets, the solar system also contains smaller objects. Asteroids are rocky objects that orbit the Sun, mostly found in the asteroid belt between Mars and Jupiter. Comets are icy bodies that come from the outer regions of the solar system and develop a glowing tail as they approach the Sun.
除了行星，太陽系還包含較小的物體。小行星是圍繞太陽運行的岩石物體，主要分布在火星和木星之間的小行星帶中。彗星是來自太陽系外區域的冰體，當它們靠近太陽時會發展出一條發光的尾巴。
Studying the solar system helps scientists understand the formation and evolution of planets, the possibility of life beyond Earth, and the dynamics of our universe.
研究太陽系有助於科學家了解行星的形成和演化，地球之外的生命可能性以及我們宇宙的動力學。
As we continue to explore and learn about the solar system, new discoveries and exciting mysteries are waiting to be revealed.
隨著我們繼續探索和學習太陽系，新的發現和令人興奮的神秘正等待著被揭示。

The Impact of Climate Change on Biodiversity

氣候變遷對生物多樣性的影響

Climate change is a pressing issue that has significant consequences for biodiversity worldwide. As the Earth's climate continues to warm, it disrupts ecosystems and threatens the survival of numerous species.
氣候變遷是一個迫切的問題，對全球生物多樣性產生重大影響。隨著地球氣候持續升溫，它破壞生態系統，威脅著眾多物種的生存。
Rising temperatures have led to changes in weather patterns and shifts in habitats. Many plant and animal species are struggling to adapt to these rapid changes. For example, polar bears are losing their sea ice habitats, making it difficult for them to hunt for food. Coral reefs, known as the rainforests of the sea, are experiencing bleaching events due to increased water temperatures, causing the loss of vibrant marine life.
升高的溫度導致了天氣模式的變化和棲息地的轉移。許多植物和動物物種正在努力適應這些快速變化。例如，北極熊失去了海冰棲息地，使它們難以覓食。珊瑚礁被稱為海洋的熱帶雨林，由於水溫升高而出現了漂白事件，導致豐富的海洋生物損失。
The impact of climate change on biodiversity extends beyond individual species. It affects the intricate web of interactions between organisms in ecosystems. As species face challenges in their survival, it can disrupt the balance of predator-prey relationships, pollination processes, and the overall functioning of ecosystems.
氣候變遷對生物多樣性的影響不僅僅局限於個別物種。它影響著生態系統中生物之間錯綜複雜的相互作用網絡。當物種面臨生存挑戰時，這可能破壞掠食者與被掠食者之間的平衡、授粉過程以及生態系統的整體運作。
Furthermore, climate change exacerbates existing threats to biodiversity, such as habitat destruction and pollution. These combined factors put additional pressure on vulnerable species and increase the risk of extinction.
此外，氣候變遷加劇了對生物多樣性的現有威脅，如棲息地破壞和污染。這些綜合因素對脆弱物種施加了額外壓力，增加了物種滅絕的風險。
Addressing climate change and protecting biodiversity requires global collaboration and concerted efforts. Conservation measures, such as the establishment of protected areas, sustainable land and resource management practices, and reducing greenhouse gas emissions, are crucial steps towards mitigating the impacts of climate change on biodiversity.
應對氣候變遷並保護生物多樣性需要全球合作和協同努力。保護措施，如建立保護區、可持續土地和資源管理實踐以及減少溫室氣體排放，是減輕氣候變遷對生物多樣性影響的關鍵步驟。
It is essential for individuals, communities, and governments to prioritize environmental stewardship and take proactive measures to reduce their carbon footprint. By promoting sustainability, supporting conservation initiatives, and raising awareness about the importance of biodiversity, we can work towards a more resilient and thriving planet.
個人、社區和政府有必要優先考慮環境保護，採取積極措施減少碳足跡。通過促進可持續發展、支持保育倡議並提高人們對生物多樣性重要性的意識，我們可以共同努力實現一個更具彈性和繁榮的地球。
